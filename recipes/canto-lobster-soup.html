Lobster Poached in Chicken Soup (上汤焗龙虾)

Reference: source

Description

The white and tender lobster with a delicious taste is served with soup stock.

Ingredients

  • 6 quick-frozen lobster tails (thin)
  • 4 slices of ginger
  • 1 spring onion
  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch
  • Marinade:
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
    • sesame oil
    • little pepper
  • Soup:
    • 1/3 cup of chicken broth
    • 1/2 teaspoon each of sugar and sesame oil
    • 1 teaspoon each of light soy sauce and cornstarch water

Steps

  1. Thaw and rinse the lobster tails , cut them into pieces, mix in the marinade for 5 minutes, and then pat them with a thin layer of cornstarch.
  2. Slightly pat the ginger slices, and cut the green onions into sections.
  3. Heat up 4-6 tablespoons of oil, fry the lobster pieces in medium hot oil until golden brown, and take out.
  4. Use the remaining oil to sauté the ginger and onion until fragrant, return the lobster to the pot, praise the wine, add the gravy and bake for 1 minute.
  5. Finally, stir in the cornstarch and water, and enjoy while it is still hot.
